About Us

The Books and Balls Initiative was founded on the 4th of August 2025 to uplift children in orphanages and poverty-stricken areas. We combine education, nutrition, and sports to create safe environments where children in South Africa can learn, grow, and thrive — building a brighter future for generations to come.

Vision

A South Africa where every child has access to quality education, proper nutrition, and safe spaces for sport.

Mission

Empowering orphaned and underprivileged children through volunteer-led education, food support, and structured sporting programmes that keep them safe from substance abuse.

Values

Education • Integrity • Discipline • Community • Hope

More About Our Work

We provide tutoring, study resources, pop-up libraries, and reading clubs in under-resourced communities.
Children receive meal packs and healthy snacks during school and sports days.
Through structured sports coaching, we build teamwork, discipline, confidence, and physical health.
