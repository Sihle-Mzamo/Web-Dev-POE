About Us

The Books and Balls Initiative was founded on the 4th of August 2025, our NPO uplifts children in orphanages and poverty-stricken areas. By combining education, nutrition, and sports to create safe environments for children in South Africa to learn, grow and thrive. Which leads to creating a better and a much brighter future for children in South Africa and soon to children all over the world.

Vision

A South Africa where every child has access to quality education, proper nutrition, and safe spaces for sport.

Mission

Empower orphaned and underprivileged children through volunteer-led education, food support, and structured sports keeping them away from any sort of substance abuse.

Values

Education • Integrity • Discipline • Community • Hope
